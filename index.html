About Panda Café

History

Panda Café was founded in October 1998 and successfully listed in China in June 2016. Its retail products include more than 30 of the world's top coffee beans, hand-made espresso and a variety of coffee hot and cold drinks, fresh and delicious variety of pastry foods, as well as a wide variety of coffee machines, coffee cups and other goods.
Panda Café is now expanding its global market investment and will rapidly expand its brand to become the world's leading organic and fair trade coffee retail brand!

Branch

Panda Café uses fair trade organic coffee beans to freshly grind, expresses the most sincere gratitude and direct support to coffee growers. It advocates craftsmanship and original ecology, and maximizes the use of recyclable materials to actively integrate into the community and local characteristics. Panda Café, located in China, New Zealand, Canada, the United Kingdom and other countries. At present, there are 200 branches in China, and the scent of Panda Café can be tasted in Beijing, Shanghai, Liaoning, Hebei, Chongqing and other 15 provinces.

Superiority

Panda Café is positioned by college students, business people and urban white-collar workers who focus on organic, health, quality, environmental protection and other fashion life concepts. The distinctive business philosophy highlights the brand advantages of Esquires Coffee: (1) Only sells healthy, environmentally friendly, pure and fashionable manually brew coffee. (2) The only international brand in Shenyang that only uses New Zealand-certified fair trade coffee beans. (3) The first coffee brand to achieve full control of the coffee making process.
